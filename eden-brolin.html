Eden Brolin is an American actress widely popular worldwide for her character in the television series Beyond as Charlie Singer. She has also appeared in Yellowstone as Mia. 

She was born on November 30, 1994, in Los Angeles, California, the United States of America.

Brolin Parents Are Famous Personalities

Beyond actress Brolin was born to her parents Josh Brolin and Alice Adair. Her father is an American actor who is widely popular for his role as Thanos in Avengers: Infinity War.

Similarly, Brolin's mother is also an actress by profession. Her notable works are Beverly Hills Cop II, Quantum Leap, and Szuler.

Are Brolin's Parents Still Together?

Brolin's parents first meet each other in the shoot of Private Eye. In the TV series, Alice played the role of Jessi, and Josh played the character of Jonny Betts. 

We can see the sizzling chemistry between Jessi and Jonny in the two parts of the episodes of Blue Hotel. From there, they get close and stay in a romantic relationship.

They legally married when Adair was twenty-two and Brolin was twenty years old. However, Brolin's parents are no longer together because they divorced in 1994 after completing six years of married life together.

After their divorce, they still chose to stay together for the sake of their children, as they did not want their children to suffer drastic upheaval in their life.
