Biography

Kenan Ece was born in Dubai, United Arab Emirates on December 24, 1980. Turkish actor who is known for both his recurring roles in television and feature film appearances. He is best recognized for having played Murat Unal in the 2019 series Avlu as well as Hakan in the 2011 film Beni Unutma. He shared the leading role with Tuba Buyukustun in the 2009 Turkish feature film Ask Your Heart. He attended Davidson College in the United States where he studied both Theater and Economics. Upon graduating, he moved to Dublin where he attended the Gaeity School of Acting and the Irish Film Academy. He then started his acting career on the Irish soap opera Fair City.
