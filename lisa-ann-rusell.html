Lisa Ann Rusell is happily married to Jeff Probst. While she has been with Probst for over a decade, she was previously married to Mark-Paul Gosselaar. Life with Probst:

Like Russell, Probst has a divorce in common but has no children from his ex-wife Shelly Wright. However, for the time being, the pair kept the details of their wedding a secret only to reveal it later in 2012.

In over a decade of marriage, the pair has not given birth to any children while Probst is stepfather to two of Rusell’s children from her previous relationship. Talking about his relationship with kids Probst is told:

“We’re a blended family, so they are technically—not technically, they are my stepkids—I think of them as mine, which is crazy, but then they have this other family…but I always have to remember they have a dad.”

Despite having little to no hiccups in their marriage, things took an awkward turn in 2017 when Rusell wanted Probst to quit the survivor series. It was later revealed that Rusell wanted more help with the kids and while Probst never left the show it is safe to say that the duo reached an amicable solution.
