OC Parks free Sunset Cinema announces summer film series lineup

 Posted on July 23, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  311 words  |  Martina Birk

OC Parks has announced the full line-up for its 2021 Sunset Cinema film series, which will take place Friday nights starting June 18-Sept. 10.

Event sites for the outdoor movies will open at 6 p.m. and films will begin at 8 p.m. Each screening is family-friendly, free and includes free parking. Guests can bring in their own picnic items, low-back chairs and blankets for seating. Food trucks and booths will be available on-site with bites and beverages for purchase. Guests 21-and-older will be able to bring in their own alcoholic beverages to enjoy within the venue space.

Here is the line-up of movies in the Sunset Cinema film series.

June 18: “The NeverEnding Story” at Craig Regional Park

June 25: “How to Train Your Dragon” at Carbon Canyon Regional Park

July 2: “Independence Day” at Carbon Canyon Regional Park

July 9: “Doolittle” at Yorba Regional Park

July 16: “Sandlot” at Yorba Regional Park

July 23: “Toy Story 4” at Mason Regional Park

July 30: “Willow” at Mason Regional Park

Aug. 6: “The Lion King” at Irvine Regional Park

Aug. 13: “The Mighty Ducks” at Irvine Regional Park

Aug. 20: “Star Wars: The Rise of Skywalker” at Laguna Niguel Regional Park

Aug. 27: “Hook” at Laguna Niguel Regional Park

Sept. 3: “Dolphin Tale” at Bluff Park at Salt Creek Beach

Sept. 10: “The Little Mermaid” at Bluff Park at Salt Creek Beach

Craig Regional Park is located at 3300 N. State College Blvd., Fullerton; Mason Regional Park, 18712 University Dr., Irvine; Irvine Regional Park, 1 Irvine Park Road, Orange; Carbon Canyon Regional Park, 4442 Carbon Canyon Road, Brea; Yorba Regional Park, 7600 E. La Palma Ave., Anaheim; Bluff Park at Salt Creek Beach, 33333 Pacific Coast Highway, Dana Point; Laguna Niguel Regional Park, 28241 La Paz Road, Laguna Niguel.

For more information, go to ocparks.com.
