The Meaning Behind The Song: Healing Is Here (Live) by BJ Putnam

Introduction

Music has a way of touching our souls in a profound way, often conveying messages that resonate deeply within us. One such song that carries a powerful message of hope and healing is “Healing Is Here (Live)” by BJ Putnam. This song speaks to the unwavering faith and trust in God’s ability to bring healing and freedom into our lives.

The Lyrics and Their Significance

The lyrics of “Healing Is Here (Live)” capture the essence of seeking and receiving divine healing. BJ Putnam’s powerful vocals, along with Nicole Binion’s harmonies, amplify the emotion and conviction behind the words.

Verse 1: Belief and Reception

In the first verse, BJ Putnam sings, “Healing is here, healing is here. Healing is here and I receive it. Healing is here, healing is here. Healing is here and I believe it.” These words emphasize the power of faith and the act of receiving healing. By acknowledging that healing is present and believing in its possibility, the song encourages listeners to open their hearts to the miracle of physical, emotional, and spiritual healing.

Chorus: Looking to God for Healing

The chorus echoes the sentiment of seeking help from a higher power. The lyrics say, “I reach my hands to the heavens. I lift my eyes where my help comes from. I look to You, my rock, my healer. I trust in You.” This chorus serves as a reminder that true healing comes from placing our trust in God, who is our source of strength and deliverance.

Verse 2: Freedom and Belief

Nicole Binion continues the song in the second verse, affirming, “Freedom is here, freedom is here. Freedom is here, and I receive it. Freedom is here, freedom is here. Freedom is here, and I believe it.” These words remind us that healing encompasses not only physical restoration but also emotional and spiritual liberation.

Bridge: Love Conquers Fear

The bridge of the song declares, “Sickness can’t stay any longer. Your perfect love is casting out fear. You are the God of all power, and it is Your will that my life is healed.” This section emphasizes the idea that God’s love is a potent force capable of dispelling fear and eradicating sickness. It reaffirms our belief that healing is aligned with God’s will and that His power can overcome any ailment or obstacle.

Personal Experience with “Healing Is Here (Live)”

This song holds a special place in my heart as I have personally experienced the profound impact of healing in my life. There have been moments when I felt beaten down by sickness and despair, but this song served as a constant reminder of the hope and restoration that God offers. Whenever I listen to “Healing Is Here (Live),” I am reminded to lift my hands and eyes to God, trusting in His perfect love and power to bring healing into my life.

Listening to this song has also helped me find solace during challenging times. The lyrics became a source of comfort and encouragement, reinforcing my faith in God’s ability to heal and restore. It has reminded me that God’s love and power are greater than any sickness or fear, instilling in me the confidence to believe in His healing for myself and others.

Conclusion

“Healing Is Here (Live)” by BJ Putnam is more than just a song; it is a powerful declaration of faith and belief in God’s healing power. Through its lyrics, this song encourages us to place our trust in God as our healer and to believe in His ability to bring healing and freedom into our lives. Whether struggling with physical ailments, emotional wounds, or spiritual battles, this song reminds us that healing is available to all who trust in God’s love and power.

Listening to this song has been a transformative experience for many, including myself, and continues to inspire hope and faith in the face of adversity. As we lift our hands and voices in worship, may we all find solace and strength in knowing that God has never failed and that He never will.
