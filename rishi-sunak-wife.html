Who Is Rishi Sunaks Wife Akshata Murty? All You Need To Know!

 Posted on August 31, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  583 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Before, a lot of attention was paid to his partner, Akshata Murty. Her tax situation was looked at closely when it was discovered that, as a non-domiciled UK resident, she paid little tax on her income. Here is all you need to know about the new Prime Minister’s wife.

Rishi Sunak is a British politician who was born on May 12, 1980. He is the next prime minister of the UK and has been the leader of the Conservative Party since October 24, 2022. He was previously Chief Secretary to the Treasury from 2019 to 2020 and Chancellor of the Exchequer from 2020 to 2022. Since 2015, he has been the MP for the area of Richmond (Yorks).

Who Did Rishi Sunak Get Married To?

Akshata Narayan Murty is an Indian businesswoman who lives in the United Kingdom. She is the daughter of N. R. Narayana Murthy and Sudha Murty. N. R. Narayana Murthy is one of the founders of the Indian multinational IT company Infosys. She owns 0.93% of Infosys, which makes her one of the richest women in Britain.

How Did Akshata Murty Meet Rishi Sunak And When Did They Marry?

Murty and Sunak met while they were both getting a Master of Business Administration at Stanford University in the United States (MBA).

In August 2009, they got married in Bangalore, India, in a big, expensive ceremony that lasted two days.

What’s Akshata Murty’s Job?

Murty used to work in finance and marketing, but when she started the fashion label Akshata Designs, which focuses on working with artists in remote Indian villages, she switched to fashion. In 2012, the label shut down.

She is now a director of Catamaran Ventures, a venture capital firm that her father started in 2010. She was in charge of setting up a branch of the investment company in the UK, which included opening an office in London.

He also has a share in the restaurants owned by Jamie Oliver in the UK and in Wendy’s chain in India.

How Many Children Do Rishi Sunak And Akshata Murty Have?

The couple has two daughters, Krishna and Anoushka.

They are mostly kept out of the public eye, so not much is known about them. However, the Prime Minister of the UK says on his “About Me” page that they keep him “busy and entertained.”

How Much Money Does Akshata Murty Have?

By anyone’s standards, Skshata Murty and her family are very rich. Just the 0.91 percent share she is said to have in her family’s tech business is worth £430 million by itself.

Just for the tax year 2021-2022, she got about £11.5 million from Infosys.

The Bloomberg Billionaires Index says that her total net worth is around $1.2 billion, which is a lot more than King Charles III.

Where Does Akshata Murty Currently Live?

Rishi Sunak’s new job means that the couple has to move to 10 Downing Street, but they have a lot of other properties that they can use when they need to get away.

They have a townhouse with five bedrooms and a flat in London, as well as a luxurious family manor house in Kirby Sigston, North Yorkshire, that sits on 12 acres of land.

There’s also the small matter of a large property in Santa Monica, California, which is a bit further away.
