Where was Robb Wells born? Ethnicity, Nationality, Family, Education

Robb Wells was born in Moncton, New Brunswick, Canada on March 20, 1971. His father was an RCMP forensic specialist. When he was eight years old, he and his family relocated to Dartmouth, Nova Scotia.
